Biography

Hello! I’m Alfa Yohannis, a lecturer at Pradita University in Indonesia. My research spans various fields, including Model-Driven Engineering, Software Engineering, Information Systems, Gamification, Decentralized Systems, Blockchain, and Computer Science Education. I earned my PhD from the University of York, UK, and served as a research fellow for 4 years there, including one-year KTP associate at Leonardo Ltd, a multinational defense company. Prior to my doctoral studies, I gained extensive experience as a software developer and lecturer, specializing in business software development and higher education.
